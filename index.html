Rainy Sunday

On a wet Sunday with not much chance to go outside, not to mind play tennis I decided to lookup the other players in my new group. Decided to make the task a bit more interesting by scrapping and visualising the members of Highbury Fields leagues.

The resulting data might not be 100% accurate but as far as I can see it is ok. Note I have only scrapped league data from Group 40 up to the last finished group.
