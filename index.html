LocalTennisLeague Stats

Welcome to LocalTennisLeagues statistic page.

As a small project I scrapped the tennis leagues website data and created this site to visualise the match results because well I really like graphs, stats and the like.

The resulting data might not be 100% accurate but as far as I can see it is ok. Note I have only scrapped league data from Group 40 up to the last finished group. Also I may expand it to include dates, player notes history and more charts.

I may run it against other leagues in future. Just Highbury Fields for now :) If you have taken part in localtennisleagues please pass it on to your friends who have also played.
